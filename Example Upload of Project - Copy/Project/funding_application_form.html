GOV FUNDING Success Stories

In 2018, McDonalds received a grant worth approximately £20,000 from GOV FUNDING. McDonalds had a project where they aimed to digitalise their operations. With our help, they implemented an online booking system with our funding. With our help McDonalds has seen profits soar and demand for their products at an all time high.

In 2019, Nick's Diner was very close to falling into administration. After reaching out and applying to GOV FUNDING, Nick's Diner is going stronger than ever, doubling their profits and revenue from previous years. GOV FUNDING ensured that Nick's Diner remained open for trading.
