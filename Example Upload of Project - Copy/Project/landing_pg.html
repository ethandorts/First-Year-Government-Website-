Apply For Funding

The government are offering small businesses an opportunity to avail of funding in order to support a digitalisation project within their company. With the current economic crisis and the aftermath of the Covid-19 pandemic, the government recognises the impact and devastation that small and local businesses are currently dealing with. In this tough climate, it is the government’s aim to reward struggling businesses, in desperate need of funding, to upscale their technologies and processes.

What is the aim of the project?

It is our aim that successful applicants of the grant would avail of new modern digital technologies to change their business model and provide new revenue and value-producing opportunities. With digitalisation, it is our hope that businesses will have a competitive advantage by doing things better, faster and cheaper than other competitors in the market.

What would digitalisation mean for your company?

  1. 1. Introduction of the use of collaborative and digital tools.
  2. 2. The use of digital files on the cloud, with user authentication methods. Protects sensitive information and improves workflows.
  3. 3. The introduction of online sales channels, known as e-commerce, integrated with management software.
  4. 4. Platforms for creating, editing and storing files and sending them to your customers. e.g bills and invoices.
  5. 5. The use of data collection and predictive analysis tools e.g. works out the most successful selling items of a shop.

What does the application process involve?

1

Register and Enrolment

Create an Account through our government website. You can create an account here

2

Express of Interest

Complete an application providing details of your business and why you believe your business is eligible for a grant.

3

Provide Supporting Documentation

If your project meets eligibility criteria, you will be asked to provide supporting evidence.

4

Selection

Provided that your checks are approved, your project will be reviewed by our grant panel and board members will notify you of the result of your application.
