How can we help you today?

Most Asked Questions

  • Applications opened on the 1st November 2022 for all small businesses and your business can apply for funding right now.

  • All companies must have submitted their applications by 16th Deecember 2022 by 23:59. Applications will close at this time and all applications submitted after this time will be not be reviewed for progression to the next stage of the application process.

  • This funding is being made available to small businesses who are in desperate need of a digitilisation project. After the hardships and difficulties of the past few years, we want to help struggling businesses recover and reach new targets.

  • After determining the need for funding in your business, our panel will decide on the monetary amount that your business requires on a variety of factors and you will be notified of this overall total amount.

  • The business applying for funding must have been founded at least 2 years ago in order to be eligible for the digitalisation funding.

  • If you have any further questions or queries, you can ring our telephone number 02871623403 or email us directly at questions@govfunding.co.uk.

  • Upon the deadline on the 16th December 2022, within two weeks, you will be notified if your business was successful in progressing to the next stage of the funding process through email.

  • All small businesses in England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland are eligible to apply for the grant. At this present moment, unfortunately, businesses in the Republic of Ireland will not be eligible for the funding grant.
